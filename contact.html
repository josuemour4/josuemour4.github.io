About me

Hello, my name is Josue!

I'm a 36-year-old Brazilian, married and father of a beautiful girl. I have lived in Australia for approximately 6 years. I arrived here with the intention of improving my English, but today my goal is to build a solid future in this country for my family.

I work as a registered electrician in the state of NSW and am proud of my profession. I am in love with my daughter and wife and I love enjoying every second of leisure with them. Technology and football are my other passions, which allow me to relax and connect with my hobbies.
